Account and Data Deletion

How to delete your ShelfQuest account, what data is deleted, and how long residual copies may remain in backups.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open the ShelfQuest app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy.
  3. Select “Delete Account”.
  4. Confirm your password and submit the request.

If you can’t access the app, use the web request option below.

Request deletion outside the app

Send an email from your account’s registered email address with subject “Delete my ShelfQuest account” to:
info@shelfquest.org

Include:

  • Your account email
  • Confirmation you want your account and data deleted

We’ll verify ownership and process your request.

What we delete

  • Account profile: name, email, and authentication identifiers
  • User settings and preferences
  • Library data you created: uploaded books and related metadata
  • Reading activity: progress, bookmarks, and session history
  • Notes, highlights, and annotations
  • Gamification data: achievements, goals, points, and activity records
  • Any other content you directly stored with ShelfQuest

What may be retained

  • Legal or security records that we are required to keep (e.g., fraud prevention, audit trails)
  • Aggregated or de‑identified analytics that cannot reasonably identify you
  • Encrypted backup copies that will be purged automatically after the retention window

Timing and retention periods

  • Production systems: account and associated content are removed within 7 days of verification
  • Backups: residual encrypted copies are overwritten within 30 days through routine rotation
  • Security logs: retained up to 90 days, then deleted or anonymized unless a longer period is legally required

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion, contact us at info@shelfquest.org.
