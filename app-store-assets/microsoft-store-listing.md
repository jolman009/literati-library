**Your Personal Library, Supercharged with AI**

ShelfQuest is the all-in-one digital library companion that transforms how you read, organize, and engage with books. Upload your EPUB and PDF collection, track reading sessions, take smart notes, and let AI-powered insights deepen your understanding — all in a beautiful, modern interface.

- Freemium model with 5 free AI calls/month, unlimited with Pro

---

**KEY FEATURES**

**AI Literary Mentor**
Go beyond just reading. ShelfQuest's Literary Mentor engages you in Socratic discussions about your books, generates comprehension quizzes, and provides AI-powered reading insights. Powered by GPT-4o-mini, it adapts to your reading level and highlights connections across your library.

**Complete Digital Library**
Upload and organize your EPUB and PDF books with automatic cover art and metadata. Browse your collection in a beautiful grid or list view. Search, filter, and sort across your entire library instantly.

**Smart Reading Sessions**
Track every reading session with detailed analytics. See your reading speed, time spent, and progress over time. Start a session from any book and pick up right where you left off.

**Gamified Reading Experience**
Earn points, unlock achievements, and maintain reading streaks. Level up as you read more. Complete daily challenges and set personal reading goals. Compete with yourself and watch your stats grow.

**Advanced Note-Taking**
Create rich notes tied to specific books or captured from the web. AI-enhanced note improvement polishes your thoughts. Search across all your notes and highlights. Export for external use.

**AI Book Recommendations**
Get personalized book recommendations based on your library, reading history, and preferences. Discover similar authors, thematic matches, and stretch picks that expand your horizons.

**12 Beautiful Themes**
Customize your reading experience with 12 Material Design 3 themes — from Ocean Breeze to Midnight Purple. Earn points to unlock new color palettes. Full dark mode support across every screen.

**Chrome Extension**
Clip articles, save notes, and create reading tasks from any webpage. Your web research flows directly into your ShelfQuest library. Smart Reading Queue suggests books related to what you're browsing.

**Works Everywhere**
ShelfQuest is a Progressive Web App that works seamlessly on Windows, Mac, Android, and any modern browser. Install it like a native app with offline support. Your library syncs across all your devices.

**Privacy & Security**
Your reading data is yours. Industry-standard encryption, secure cloud backup, and full data export. GDPR-compliant with transparent data handling. Delete your data anytime from Settings.
